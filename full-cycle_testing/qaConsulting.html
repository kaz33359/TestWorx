Process for consulting on quality assurance at TestWorx

We employ a tried-and-true three-step procedure for each consulting case in order to provide a thorough analysis of the current software testing workflows and suggest workable solutions for each problematic component. We also have multiple collaboration plans, each with a varied service scope, to cater to the various needs of our clients.

1. Finding

Testworx begins by doing the following:

  • • Interviewing significant parties
  • • Examine current procedures and techniques
  • • Identify shortcomings and their causes
2. Development of a strategy

Our consultants then move on to:

  • • Select the QA procedures that will be replaced or implemented.
  • • Choose a testing toolkit that is appropriate for the job.
  • • Put together a revolutionary road map
3. Recommendations for action and change

We provide three different involvement strategies for the last phase:

Testworx's full implementation
  • • joining your QA team to carry out a comprehensive transformation
  • • Introducing fresh approaches
  • • following up on how they're doing
  • • Informing your staff about the new procedures
  • • resolving any problems that may arise
shared accountability
  • • helping your internal QA staff embrace ongoing changes by working together
  • • adopting the agreed-upon job scope
  • • giving the team suggestions that can be put into practise
