Comprehensive testing of embedded software

Feature functions

By introducing pertinent test data into the system, we look for any flaws and bottlenecks.

Non-operational elements

We put embedded systems to the test in time, energy, and other resource-constrained circumstances.

Environment

Through the use of prototypes, hardware emulation, and other techniques, we evaluate the system performance in a simulated setting.

Our techniques

Black box

We check the system using a well-chosen collection of methodologies based on the specifications, executing the tests concurrently and in coordination with your development team.

White box

We thoroughly test your embedded system at every iterative stage of development to assure its integrity throughout.

Grey box

Utilizing the most effective elements of both black-box and white-box testing, we increase test coverage to encompass all possible scenarios.
