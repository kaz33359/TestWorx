How TestWorx addresses issues with blockchain testing ?

Transaction irreversibility

No error can be corrected due to the immutability of blockchain data. Therefore, before the transaction data are added to your blockchain, we ensure that they are accurate.


Expensive live testing

Blockchain testing in a real-world setting can be prohibitively expensive. To reduce related QA expenditures, we implement a testnet.


Fee-based transactions

Our duty is to ensure that all transaction-related activities in the network are error-free because every blockchain transaction has a cost.

Security

We confirm that the software, including blockchain-based web and mobile apps, is not exposed to attacks at the network, user, or mining levels.

Multiple participants involved

We employ emulators of the OSs and devices in use, as well as conduct testing on actual devices, with an infinite number of network members operating from various geographical regions.

Our services for blockchain testing

Functional Testing

Through the use of our innovative approach, we make sure that all the necessary functional ranges of fundamental blockchain product components are handled accurately.

Performance Testing

We will locate performance bottlenecks, confirm that the software runs without a hitch under certain workloads, and make sure it is prepared for production to ensure consistent quality.

Security Testing

The TestWorx specialists confirm that the system's encryption and key storage are successful at thwarting potential security intrusions. We'll evaluate the security of permission mechanisms as well.

Integration Testing

Our experts evaluate the intersystem connections' coherence and functionality across all blockchain app environments and connected parts.

Smart contract Testing

All potential outcomes in the smart contract logic will be simulated, examined, and predicted by our QA specialists, who will also perform checks while taking into account dynamically changing operational networks, code self-consistency, and invariant bricks.

Test automation

With a high number of nodes, their combinations, and validations during contract processing, as well as the quick network development and transformation, we automate testing to save time.
