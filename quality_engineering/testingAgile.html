How we as an Agile team conduct testing ?

TestWorx has created a customised testing strategy to assist clients in releasing high-quality software on schedule:

To receive a completed increment, we guarantee at the start of each sprint that all work will be completed by the end of the sprint.

We begin setting up the testing infrastructure and documentation concurrently with the development team creating new features in order to shorten release cycles.

We retain records throughout the iteration and keep the test documentation model current and optimised to support each sprint.

We do continuous testing to meet the requirements of the quality gates, which includes unit and integration testing as well as test automation inspections.

In order to reduce the amount of time spent on regression testing and put more emphasis on the exploratory testing of new feature and user experience, test automation is introduced early in the SDLC process.

When developing an all-encompassing test plan, we use the Agile testing quadrants.

Together with the development and business analytics teams, we participate in backlog refinement meetings to get clear requirements and get rid of the need for any additional revisions.

We do retrospectives and sprint reviews to consider the results of the sprint and pinpoint areas for improvement.
